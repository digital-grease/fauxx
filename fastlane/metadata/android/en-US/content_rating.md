# IARC Content Rating Questionnaire - Expected Responses

Reference document for completing the Google Play Console content rating questionnaire.

## General Questions

- **Does the app contain violence?** No
- **Does the app contain sexual content or nudity?** No
- **Does the app contain profanity or crude humor?** No
- **Does the app contain controlled substances (drugs, alcohol, tobacco)?** No
- **Does the app contain gambling?** No
- **Does the app contain user-generated content?** No (all content is system-generated)
- **Does the app allow users to communicate with each other?** No
- **Does the app share the user's location with other users?** No
- **Does the app allow purchases of digital goods?** No
- **Does the app contain ads?** No

## Expected Rating

Based on the above responses, the expected IARC rating is:
- **ESRB:** Everyone
- **PEGI:** 3
- **USK:** 0
- **GRAC:** All Ages
- **IARC:** 3+

## Notes for Reviewer

- The app generates synthetic web browsing activity for privacy protection
- No user-facing content is violent, sexual, or otherwise objectionable
- The app does not display web content to the user — all browsing happens in background WebViews
- No in-app purchases, no ads, no social features
