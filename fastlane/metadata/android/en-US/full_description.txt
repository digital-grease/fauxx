Fauxx is an open-source privacy tool that protects you by generating diverse synthetic browsing activity in the background. By flooding data brokers and ad-tech platforms with plausible off-demographic noise, Fauxx makes your real behavioral signal statistically indistinguishable from fake data.

HOW IT WORKS

Fauxx runs quietly in the background and performs diverse browsing activities across many topic categories — searches, page visits, DNS lookups, and fingerprint rotation. The goal is to bury your real interests under a mountain of synthetic noise so that data brokers can't build an accurate profile of you.

FEATURES

• Diverse search queries across Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, and Yahoo on topics far from your real interests
• Varied website visits across dozens of content categories to broaden your browsing profile
• Browser fingerprint rotation (User-Agent, language, and other headers)
• DNS query diversification to obscure your browsing patterns at the network level
• Smart targeting engine that weights noise AWAY from your real demographics
• Synthetic persona rotation — the app adopts a new fake identity every week for temporal coherence
• Poisson-distributed timing that mimics natural human browsing patterns
• Full audit log of all synthetic activity
• On-device encryption for all personal data (SQLCipher + Android Keystore)
• No accounts, no cloud storage, no analytics, no telemetry

OPTIONAL DEMOGRAPHIC TARGETING

Tell Fauxx about yourself (age, gender, interests, profession, region) and it will generate noise that's maximally different from your real profile. All demographic data stays encrypted on your device and is never transmitted. Skip the setup entirely if you prefer uniform random noise.

PRIVACY FIRST

• All data stays on your device — nothing is ever sent to our servers (we don't have servers)
• No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising identifiers
• Fully open-source — inspect the code yourself
• Clear All Data option to wipe everything instantly

BATTERY & DATA CONSCIOUS

• WiFi-only mode to avoid mobile data usage
• Configurable battery threshold — pauses automatically when battery is low
• Adjustable intensity (Low / Medium / High)
• Active hours scheduling — runs only when you want it to

Fauxx is free, open-source, and contains no ads. Your privacy is not for sale.
